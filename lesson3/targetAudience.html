Target Audience:

The target audience is men and women between the ages of 15 and 65, living in Europe or the US with different professions or studies.
They are used to the use of tablets, smartphones, and computers. They will use the application to organize their schedule, dress for the
weather, and avoid any inconvenience due to the weather.

Persona 1

Fictional name: Madison Wright
Occupation: CEO on a Postal Enterprise.
Demographics and Education:
  • Divorced
  • 40 years old
  • Mother of one child
  • Master’s in Business Administration
    		•
    Goals and motivation for using the site: Madison uses her phone to plan the schedule for her company's drivers. The packages are delivered with great care. She always tries to prevent her employees from making deliveries when it rains to avoid car accidents.
    Interests: Drive her son to the school, talk with her workers, follow the shipments, lover of family time.
    Technology: Smartphone, Laptop, and GPS’s car. She uses her phone to contact the enterprise and her son. She does not have enough time to drive carefully when it is raining. She needs an app to help her to program her schedule for the week and drive without rain.
    Quote: “My son and my job are first.”
